Discover new drugs with DOCK6

DOCK6 is a molecular modeling program used to identify potential binding geometries and interactions of a molecule to a target. Anchor-and-grow is the main algorithm that samples many structural poses. The above animation is an illustration of how anchor-and-grow would look like if DOCK6 was running. While a library of molecules is sampling against the target site, DOCK6 can score each molecule on-the-fly with energy and shape-based scoring functions. It features a full-fledged docking software, where calculations are done at the terminal.
